The International Organization for Standardization?

What is ISO?

ISO is how sensitive a camera is to light. Turning this down will allow the camera to respond to more light. If it's higher, then the camera will be able to take photos in darker areas, but you will notice a grainy effect on the image.
Because of this, photographers tend to try and change the Aperature and Shutter Speed before chaning the ISO.
