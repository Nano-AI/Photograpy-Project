What is shutter speed?


The shutter speed is the speed at which the shutter closes and opens to take a photo.
The smaller a shutter speed, the faster the shutter closes.
Shorter shutter speeds can be used to take still photos of moving things to capture a "still in time" effect.
Using a higher shutter speed will let in more light, while using a smaller one will let in less.
